# My Duino-Coin Journey

## What Is Duino-Coin?

Duino-Coin is a lightweight cryptocurrency designed for microcontrollers, single-board computers, and embedded systems.

Unlike traditional cryptocurrency mining, Duino-Coin allows devices such as:
- ESP32
- Arduino
- Raspberry Pi
- ESP8266

to participate in mining and distributed computing experiments.

The project focuses on accessibility, education, electronics, and embedded systems learning.
